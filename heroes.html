Hero Archives

Alex

Origin: Earth Era
Era: Earth Era to Imperial Era
Title: Grand Emperor of the Thiran Empire

The Architect of Empire. Alex is the central figure around whom the fate of the Milky Way rotates. Beginning as a survivor in the desperate final days of Earth, his trajectory from a human leader to the immortal Grand Emperor is the backbone of the saga.

A visionary of cold, pragmatic genius, Alex realized early on that for life to survive the chaos of the cosmos, it required a "Blanket" of absolute order.

He is the architect of the Three Fundamental Laws and the founder of Thira. While often viewed as a cold autocrat, Alex’s motivations are rooted in a deep-seated obsession with evolution and the preservation of sentient life against external threats—be they rogue planets or ancient, hidden masters.

Jain

Origin: Earth Era
Era: Earth Era to Imperial Era
Title: The Imperial Confidante / Keeper of the Core

The Anchor of Humanity. Jain stands as the essential counterbalance to Alex’s grand ambitions. From the earliest days of Project Gaia, she was the one who could see the man behind the Emperor.

Where Alex looks at the galaxy in terms of logistics, tithes, and evolution, Jain remains connected to the individual cost of those decisions.

As one of the few humans to bridge the gap between the death of Earth and the height of the Empire, she serves as a repository of human history and emotion. In the Imperial Era, her presence is a stabilizing force within Alex’s inner circle, often acting as the silent conscience of the throne.

Bara

Origin: Earth (Jordan)

Commander of Military Operations

A man of iron discipline and tactical foresight. In the chaos following the Earth’s impact, Bara was the wall between order and extinction. He is the silent architect of Thira’s security and power infrastructure.

Clive

Origin: Earth (England)

Elite Pilot & Survival Navigator

The heartbeat of the journey to Gaia. A pilot whose nerves never frayed during the Great Impact, he was responsible for the harrowing transport of the "chosen" to the safety of the stars.

Dimitri

Origin: Earth (Russia)

Lead Historian & Cultural Archivist

The guardian of collective memory. Dimitri ensures that while humans become Thiran, they do not forget the lessons of the old Earth, translating past failures into the laws of the new Empire.

Eldon

Origin: Earth (England)

Chief Communications Officer

The voice in the dark. Eldon managed the fragile threads of human connection during the early days of Gaia. He is the filter through which all news—good or catastrophic—must pass.

Habo

Origin: Alpaz / Orcish Lineage

Tribal Leader & First Contact Catalyst

A bridge between the Thiran "Circle" and indigenous species. His leadership proved that the Empire could integrate diverse biologies through respect rather than just conquest.

Hypatia

Origin: Earth (Greece)

Chief Astrophysicist

Named after the ancient scholar, she maps the heavens. Her calculations allowed the Gaia mission to navigate Oort cloud anomalies, turning mathematical certainty into Imperial expansion.

Konoya

Origin: Earth (Japan)

Lead Psychologist

The architect of the Thiran psyche. Konoya specializes in mitigating the "Apostasis" that breaks lesser men, ensuring the pioneers remain focused, sane, and loyal to the cause.

Pars

Origin: Gaia / First Generation

Resource Logistics & Planetary Scout

A specialist in the Nature & Resources division. One of the first to walk Gaia's soil, he identified the mineral wealth that fueled the construction of the massive Thiran Armada.

Prana (Prasi)

Origin: Earth (Lineage) / Thira

Imperial Ambassador

The diplomatic face of the Empire. She ensures First Contact follows the Thiran Tithe, allowing species their culture while demanding their 15% wealth and total loyalty to Alex.

Steve

Origin: Earth (South Africa)

Head of Applied Engineering

The man who turned theory into titanium. Steve led the teams that built the first permanent structures on Gaia and the orbital stations above Thira. His fingerprints are on every blueprint.
