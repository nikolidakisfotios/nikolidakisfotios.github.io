Hero Archives

Alex

Origin: Earth Era

Grand Emperor of Thira

The Architect of Empire. Alex transitioned from a human leader to an immortal autocrat, driven by a pragmatic obsession with the preservation of sentient life.

Jain

Origin: Earth Era

The Imperial Confidante

The Anchor of Humanity. Jain serves as the counterbalance to Alex’s grand ambitions, acting as the repository of human emotion and the silent conscience of the throne.

Bara

Origin: Earth (Jordan)

Commander of Military Operations

A man of iron discipline and tactical foresight. Bara was the wall between order and extinction during Earth's impact and the architect of Thira’s security.

Clive

Origin: Earth (England)

Elite Pilot & Navigator

The heartbeat of the journey to Gaia. His nerves never frayed during the Great Impact, ensuring the harrowing transport of the "chosen" to the safety of the stars.

Dimitri

Origin: Earth (Russia)

Lead Historian

The guardian of collective memory. Dimitri ensures the lessons of the old Earth are translated into the laws of the new Empire, so humanity never forgets its roots.

Eldon

Origin: Earth (England)

Chief Communications Officer

The voice in the dark. Eldon managed the threads of human connection in the early days of Gaia, filtering the news that shaped the new civilization.
