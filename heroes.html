Alex

Origin: Earth Era
Era: Earth Era to Imperial Era
Title: Grand Emperor of the Thiran Empire

The Architect of Empire. Alex is the central figure around whom the fate of the Milky Way rotates. Beginning as a survivor in the desperate final days of Earth, his trajectory from a human leader to the immortal Grand Emperor is the backbone of the saga.

A visionary of cold, pragmatic genius, Alex realized early on that for life to survive the chaos of the cosmos, it required a "Blanket" of absolute order.

He is the architect of the Three Fundamental Laws and the founder of Thira. While often viewed as a cold autocrat, Alex’s motivations are rooted in a deep-seated obsession with evolution and the preservation of sentient life against external threats—be they rogue planets or ancient, hidden masters.

Jain

Origin: Earth Era
Era: Earth Era to Imperial Era
Title: The Imperial Confidante / Keeper of the Core

The Anchor of Humanity. Jain stands as the essential counterbalance to Alex’s grand ambitions. From the earliest days of Project Gaia, she was the one who could see the man behind the Emperor.

Where Alex looks at the galaxy in terms of logistics, tithes, and evolution, Jain remains connected to the individual cost of those decisions.

As one of the few humans to bridge the gap between the death of Earth and the height of the Empire, she serves as a repository of human history and emotion. In the Imperial Era, her presence is a stabilizing force within Alex’s inner circle, often acting as the silent conscience of the throne.
